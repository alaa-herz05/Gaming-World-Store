⚔️ Ghost of Tsushima

An open-world adventure game set on Tsushima Island during the Mongol invasion of Japan in the 13th century. You play as the warrior Jin Sakai, who fights to protect his homeland using samurai skills and stealth.

🎮 Genre: Adventure / Open World
